## Editing contract

- The first six chapters must use active browser clips, not still images.
- Every clip is re-timed from its first to last marker; frozen-frame padding is
  prohibited.
- Chapter-specific crops may remove the sidebar but must keep complete
  conversation and inspector text visible.
- Only the verification and closing chapters may use generated stills, each
  with visible camera movement.

## Compliance checklist

- Runtime is below three minutes.
- Audio explains what was built and how both Codex and GPT-5.6 were used.
- The captured runs retain three full Codex Session IDs.
- Public buyer and supplier display names are anonymized.
- The correction benchmark is visibly synthetic.
- No music, third-party logos, or copyrighted footage is used.
- The narration is English and the final video must be public on YouTube.
